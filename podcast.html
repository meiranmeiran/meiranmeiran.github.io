Faucet and Drain Science: Explaining Water, One Drop at a Time

Water is vital to human society and the planet, yet the science and engineering behind safe and reliable water systems often remain invisible to the public. This podcast consists of short, interview-style episodes produced by students in CEE 437 and CEE 538 who translate water quality science and engineering concepts into accessible conversations about everyday water issues. The project also helps students practice community-facing science communication skills that are valuable for their future careers.
