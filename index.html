Welcome

Welcome to the Mei Research Group working at the Microbiome-Environment Interface! We investigate fundamental aspects of microbial diversity, physiology, and ecology, applying this knowledge to engineered water systems, such as wastewater treatment. Our goal is to develop microbiology-informed engineering strategies that enhance system performance and mitigate the environmental impact of various waste streams.
