Welcome

Welcome to the Mei Research Group working at the Microbiome-Environment Interface! We are in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. We study microbial diversity in various engineered and natural water systems, and leverage the metabolic capabilities to enhance the sustainability of those systems. To achieve our goals, we integrate genomics, microbial evolution, microbial ecology, and engineering principles. You can learn more about our research here.
