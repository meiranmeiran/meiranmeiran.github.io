Ran Mei

Ran will join CEE at UIUC as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2024. Yes, I am coming home! I got my Ph.D. (2020) and M.S. (2015) here. I also had a B.S. (2013) from Peking University in China and postdoctoral experiences at Japan's National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST, 2021-23) and Japan Agency for Marine-Earth Science and Technology (JAMSTEC, 2023-24). My research is centered around microbial ecology in natural and engineered ecosystems, with an emphasis on promoting sustainable water systems. Teaching is another passion of mine, and I will be teaching CEE444 (Biological Principles in Envrionemntal Engineering) and CEE538 (Water Quality Control II). After work, I enjoying swimming, ping pong, cooking, and short-distance travel.
