Ran Mei

Assistant Professor

Ran joined the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign as an Assistant Professor in November 2024. A proud alumnus of the department, he earned his Ph.D. in 2020 and M.S. in 2015. Ran also holds a B.S. degree from Peking University in China (2013) and gained postdoctoral experience at AIST (2021–2023) and and JAMSTEC (2023–2024) in Japan. His research focuses on microbial ecology in natural and engineered ecosystems, with a particular emphasis on advancing sustainable water systems. In addition to research, Ran is passionate about teaching and will be instructing CEE 444: Biological Principles in Environmental Engineering and CEE 538: Water Quality Control II.
