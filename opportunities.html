Opportunities for joining us

We are always seeking self-motivated students to join our group, particularly those with strong background and/or interest in environmental microbiology and environmental engineering. To apply, please contact Dr. Ran Mei with your CV and a brief statement of your research interests.

For those considering attending our graduate program, you have to apply through CEE Illinois (find all details here). Please indicate on your application that you are interested in working with Dr. Ran Mei. Prospective graduate students are encouraged to apply for fellowships (some are listed below). It develops your scientific thinking and writing skills, and offers you greater freedom in research activities that align better with your personal academic passions.

For current undergraduates, we strongly encourage you to participate in the Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) Program. This program offers a valuable opportunity to gain hands-on research experience, setting a strong foundation for your future academic and professional career.
