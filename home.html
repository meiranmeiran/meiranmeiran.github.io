Welcome

The Mei Research Group in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign works at the Microbiome-Environment Interface. We aim to understand microbial diversity in various engineered and natural water systems, and leverage the metabolic capabilities to enhance the sustainability of those systems. To achieve our goals, we integrate genomics, microbial evolution, microbial ecology, and engineering principles. You can learn more about our research here.

Diversity is essential in the microbial world we research - often microbes with varied traits cooperate to accomplish a metabolic task that would be impossible individually. The diversity of human society, and our recognition of it, serves as a foundation for innovative ideas and creative accomplishments. Our group values diversity, equity, and inclusion, and supports students and researchers of all backgrounds and identities.
